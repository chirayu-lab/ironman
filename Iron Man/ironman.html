Tony Stark — billionaire, genius, playboy, philanthropist — became Iron Man after building a powerful suit of armor to escape captivity. What started as survival soon evolved into a mission to protect humanity. Combining cutting-edge technology, artificial intelligence, and heart, he redefined the idea of a hero.

His legacy isn’t just the armor — it’s the belief that anyone can change the world through innovation and courage.
