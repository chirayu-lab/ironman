Evolution of Iron Man’s Armor

Mark I

The first Iron Man armor, built in a cave with limited resources. Heavy, crude, and powerful enough to make an escape.

Mark III

The iconic red-and-gold armor. Equipped with repulsors, flight, and advanced targeting systems — the first true Iron Man suit.

Mark XLV

Sleek and aerodynamic, used during the Age of Ultron. Enhanced combat AI and smooth integrated armor plating.

Mark L (Nanotech)

Nanotechnology armor capable of self-repair, instant deployment, and versatile weapon systems — the pinnacle of Stark tech.
