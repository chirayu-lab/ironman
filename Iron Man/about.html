Iron Man is more than just a suit of armor — he is a symbol of innovation, determination, and human evolution. Born from tragedy, Tony Stark turned his genius into a force for good, merging man and machine into one unstoppable being.

With every new iteration of the suit, Tony pushed technology beyond imagination — from the bulky Mark I to the nanotech-powered Mark 85. His inventions redefined the battlefield and the future of AI, robotics, and energy.

Even after his sacrifice in Avengers: Endgame, his legacy continues through Stark Industries, Spider-Man’s tech, and the inspiration he left for future heroes.

"Sometimes you’ve got to run before you can walk." — Tony Stark
